Chapter 1
The Girl With Dreams
Misato, Saitama, Japan — 1988

Subah ki dhoop thi. Ek ladki cycle par ja rahi thi — hawa mein baal bikhar rahe the, Aur face par ek alag si chamak thi.

Woh Junko thi. ek ladki jiska dream tha — singer banne ka, Woh apne friend circle ki favourite girl thi. wo dekhne mein behad khubshurat thi. Phir ek din Miyano naam ka ladka usko propose karta hai but Junko usko accept nahi karti hai aur Miyano isko bardaast nahi kar sakta tha.

Junko part time job karti thi apne graduation trip ke liye. Wo apne mammy-papa, bade aur chhote bhai ke sath rahti thi. uske bhai usse tease karte the, aur woh unse nakhre karti thi. Ek adorable ladki. Ek cute si family. Ek cute si shaam.

Lekin 25 November 1988 — woh shaam cute nahi thi. Junko ko kidnap karne wale 4 ladke. Jo Ogura, Minato, Yasushi, Miyano wahi Miyano jisne Junko ko propose kiya tha. Miyano Japani maafiya jise Yakuza bhi kahte hai isse mila hua tha aur iss gang ke liye kaam karta tha. Junko ko Miyano ke baare mein ye sab nahi pata tha.

Chapter 2
The Trap
8:30 PM — The last ordinary moment

Raat ke 8:30 baj rahe the. Junko ne apni shift khatam ki thi. Cycle par baithi — ghar ja rahi thi. Thandi hawa thi. Andhera tha. Par woh darti nahi thi kyunki woh apne city ko jaanti thi.

Par woh nahi jaanti thi ki Miyano aur Minato usko ghoor rahe the.

Miyano ne Minato ko order diya — "Jaa, use cycle se gira."

Minato bhaga aur usko Dhakka de diya. Junko gir gayi. Uske ghutne chhil gaye. Cycle kharab ho gayi. Woh darr gayi.

Tabhi Miyano aaya — "Arey, tum theek ho? Main tumhe ghar chhod deta hoon."

Awaaz mein itna pyaar. Chehra mein itna bholapan. Junko ne bharosa kiya. Woh uski sabse badi galati thi.

Chapter 3
The First Scream
Warehouse · Hotel · 3 AM Park

Miyano use ek warehouse mein le gaya. Wahan usne Junko ka rape kiya. Junko ne chillaya. Koi nahi aaya.

Phir use ek hotel mein le gaya. Wahan bhi uska rape kiya. Uske baad Miyano ne apne doston ko phone kiya — "Bhai, maza aa gaya. Ab tum log bhi aao."

Phir Raat ke 3 baje, Miyano Junko ko Park mein le gaya jaha baaki 3 ladke wait kar rahe the. Unhone uska notebook dekha jisme Junko ka address tha. Unhone kaha — "Hum Yakuza hain. Agar police ko bataya, toh teri maa, baap, bhai — sab ko maar denge."

Junko ki rooh kaanp gayi wo darr gayi aur ladko ne use kaaboo mein kar liya aur le gaye.

Chapter 4
The Cage
Minato's house — 2-storey hell

Woh use le gaye — Minato ke ghar. jaha wo Upar ek room mein rahte the.

27 November ko Junko ke mammy-papa ne report darj karayi, toh Miyano ne Junko ko majboor kiya ki wo apne mammy-papa ke paas phone kare aur kahe ki woh bhaag gayi hai aur apne friend ke sath rah rahi hai aur woh safe hai.

Minato ke maa-baap neechay rehte the. Unhe pata tha ki upar kya ho raha hai. Unhone police ko call nahi kiya. Kyunki? Unka beta Minato unko maarta tha. Aur Yakuza ka dar tha.

Chapter 5
The First Week
22 — 28 November 1988

Day 1: Sabne mil kar Junko ke Kapde faad diye. usko Nanga dance karwaya. aur Masturbate karne ko majboor kiya. aur phir sabne mil kar Junko ka rape karna shuru kiya ek ke baad ek.

Day 2: Junko ko Cigarette se jala rahe the — haath, pair, pet, face sab jagah.

Day 3: Wo log Junko ko Bhookha rakhte the. usko kewal doodh peene ko dete the aur usko khudka hi urine(Peshab) peene ko majboor karte the.

Day 4: Uski Vagina(Private part) mein lohe ki rod aur plastic ki bottle daalte the aur jalti hui machis.

Day 5: uske pet par lohe ki ball se marte the. Naak mein itna khoon bhara ki woh sirf mooh se saans le paati.

Day 6: Uske Haath chhat se bandh kar punching bag banaya. Junko ke chehre ko Concrete floor par rakh kar uspe koodate the.

Day 7: Usne socha — "Aaj maut aa jaaye toh achha hai." Par maut nahi aayi. Sirf woh 4 ladke aaye.

Chapter 6
The Parade of Monsters
Day 8 — 20 · 100+ men · 500+ rapes

Agle 40 dino tak Junko ke sath daily rape hota tha, Miyano ne doston ko bulana shuru kiya. total 100 se bhi jyada alag-alag logo ne 500 baar se bhi jyada Junko ke sath rape kiya. ek baar to 12 alag-alag logo ne ek hi din mein rape kiya.

Day 11: Junko ko lohe ki chad se peeta. Anus mein patakhe daal kar jalaya. uske Nakhun tod diye. Vagina(Private part) mein cigarette daali. Sardi mein balcony par sone ko majboor kiya.

Day 15: Usne ek baar bathroom jaane ka bahana banaya. Khidki se neighbour ko aawaaz di. Neighbour ne police call ki. Police aayi. Ghar ke andar dekha. Sunaa. Aur chali gayi. "Kuch nahi hai." Woh 4 ladke hasne lage.

Day 20: Miyano uske pair ko lighter se jala raha tha. Junko chal nahi paa rahi thi. uske chehra par garam mombatti tapkaya. Cigarette lighter se palkein jalayi. uske Breast mein sui daaldi. uske left nipple ko plier se kaat diya. Vagina(Private part) mein garam bulb daala, Kainchi daali jiske wajah se vagina(Private part) se bahut jyada khoon baha bechari thik se Peshaab nahi kar paa rahi thi. ek baar toh Bathroom jaane mein 1 ghanta lagta tha woh reng kar jaati thi. Junko ko itna maarte the ki uske Kaan ke parde permanently damaged ho gaye the. uska Brain shrink ho gaya.

Chapter 7
The Breaking Point
Day 21 — 35 · The rotting body

Sab logo ne Junko ko itna torture kiya ki uska face bahut swell ho gaya tha aur Uske body se sadne ki smell aane lagi — infection, pus, rotting smell. isi wajah se woh log ab Junko se bore ho gaye the.

Unhone ek 19 saal ki aurat ko kidnap kiya — gangrape kiya — chhod diya. Kyunki Junko ab unhe ghinauni lag rahi thi.

Junko ne ek din kaha — "Mujhe maar do aur iss sab se chhutkara paao." lekin Woh log hanse. Usne dubara kaha — "Please... mujhe maar daalo..." Unhone uska mooh tape se bandh diya.

Chapter 8
The Longest Night
1 January 1989 — New Year's Eve

Andheri raat, Akela room. Junko zameen par padi thi — hil nahi paati. Uske body mein itna dard tha ki woh ro bhi nahi sakti thi.

Usne aankhein band ki — aur socha ki "Naya saal. Kya badlega?" Kuch nahi badla. Sirf uski saans aur dheemi hui.

Woh jaanti thi ki ab bas kuch din aur.

Chapter 9
The Final Day
4 January 1989 — 6 AM

Subah. Woh 4 log Mahjong khel rahe the. aur Miyano game haar gaya. aur apna saara Gussa Junko par nikaalta hai, usne Lohe ka rod uthaya. Aur Junko ki dard se bhari body par barbar maara.

Uske mooh aur naak se khoon bahane laga. Unhone wapas garam mombatti uske face aur aankhon par rakhi Lighter se Aag lagayi.

Junko 2 ghante tak jalati rahi. Woh 2 ghante tak dekhte rahe. Uske cheekhein uss andheri raat mein ghul gayi.

Phir woh chal nahi rahi thi. Unhone socha — "Mar gayi?" Nahi. Woh coma mein thi. Phir bhi unhonne Lohe ki ball pet par giraya. uske body se khoon bahne laga to usne kapde se chhupa liya, phir Unhone plastic bags haath mein daale aur phir se usko bahut maara. wo bheekh maang rahi thi ki

"Mujhe maar do... please..." — Junko...😭

2 ghante baad — Junko ki saans ruk gayi. Usne apni aakhri saans akeli li. Koi nahi tha. Sirf woh 4 the — jo dekhte rahe.

Chapter 10
The Disposal
